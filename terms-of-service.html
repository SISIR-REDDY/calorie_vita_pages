2. Description of Service

Calorie Vita is a comprehensive health and wellness mobile application designed to assist users with nutritional tracking and fitness goal management. The App is provided free of charge with no in-app purchases or subscription fees.

2.1 Core Features

  • AI-powered food recognition and nutrition analysis from photos
  • Manual food logging and calorie/macronutrient tracking
  • Barcode scanning for packaged food nutrition data
  • Fitness goal setting and progress monitoring
  • Macro and micronutrient tracking with daily analytics
  • Weight management and BMI tracking
  • Exercise logging and calorie burn tracking
  • Intermittent fasting timer with multiple protocols
  • Data export (CSV, PDF) and sharing capabilities

2.2 AI-Powered Services

  • "Trainer Sisir" AI Chatbot: An AI-powered personal nutrition and fitness coach that provides personalized guidance based on your profile, dietary data, and fitness goals.
  • Food Photo Analysis: Automated nutritional content analysis from food photographs using advanced AI models (via OpenRouter API).
  • Intelligent Recommendations: AI-driven meal suggestions, nutrition coaching, and health insights.

2.3 Health Platform Integrations

  • Apple HealthKit (iOS): Optional read-only integration for steps, calories burned, and workout data.
  • Google Health Connect (Android): Optional read-only integration for steps, calories burned, and exercise data.
