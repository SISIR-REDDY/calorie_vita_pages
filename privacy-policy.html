1. Introduction

Welcome to Calorie Vita ("we," "our," or "us"), developed and operated by SISIR Labs. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use the Calorie Vita mobile application (the "App") available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store, and our related website and services (collectively, the "Services").

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree to these terms, please do not use the App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when you register for and use the App:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password (encrypted). If you use Google Sign-In or Sign in with Apple, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from those services.
  • Health Profile Data: Height, weight, age, gender, date of birth, body measurements, fitness goals, target weight, and BMI.
  • Dietary Information: Food preferences, dietary restrictions, allergies, country of residence (for regional food recommendations), and nutritional goals (calorie and macronutrient targets).
  • Food Logs & Photos: Meal entries, food photographs captured or selected from your device for AI-powered nutrition analysis, barcode scan results, and manual food log entries.
  • Exercise Data: Exercise type, duration, and calorie burn information that you manually log.
  • Intermittent Fasting Data: Fasting protocol selection, timer data, and fasting history.
  • Weight Tracking Data: Weight entries and measurement history.
  • AI Chat Conversations: Messages sent to and received from the "Trainer Sisir" AI coaching feature, stored securely to maintain conversation context and history.
  • Support Communications: Information you provide when contacting us for customer support or feedback.

2.2 Apple HealthKit & Google Health Connect Data

Important: Calorie Vita integrates with Apple HealthKit (iOS) and Google Health Connect (Android) to provide a comprehensive health tracking experience. This integration is entirely optional and requires your explicit consent.

If you grant permission, we access the following health data in read-only mode:

  • Step count
  • Active calories burned
  • Total calories burned
  • Workout/exercise sessions and duration

How HealthKit/Health Connect Data Is Used:

  • This data is used solely within the App to display your fitness activity, calculate net calorie balance, and provide personalized AI coaching insights.
  • HealthKit and Health Connect data is not sold, shared with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes, or used for any purpose other than providing App functionality to you.
  • HealthKit data is not stored on external servers — it is cached locally on your device via secure local storage and synced only as aggregated daily summaries to your personal Firebase account for cross-device access.
  • You can revoke HealthKit/Health Connect access at any time through your device’s system settings, and the App will continue to function without this data.

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, certain information is collected automatically:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, interaction patterns, session duration, screen views, and navigation paths (collected via Firebase Analytics).
  • Crash & Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs, and diagnostic information (collected via Firebase Crashlytics) to improve App stability.
  • Camera & Photo Library Access: Photos captured or selected for food logging are processed for AI analysis. We access your camera and photo library only when you actively initiate food scanning.

2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect precise GPS location on iOS. On Android, location permissions are declared only for Health Connect integration requirements.
  • We do not track users across other apps or websites (NSPrivacyTracking is set to false).
  • We do not collect payment or financial information — the App is entirely free with no in-app purchases.
  • We do not access your contacts, call logs, or SMS messages.

3. How We Use Your Information

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

3.1 Core App Functionality

  • Provide food tracking, calorie counting, macronutrient analysis, and progress monitoring.
  • Process food photographs through AI services to identify foods and estimate nutritional content.
  • Facilitate barcode scanning to retrieve packaged food nutrition data from public food databases.
  • Synchronize your data across devices via your Firebase account.
  • Maintain your account and authenticate your identity.
  • Enable data export (CSV, PDF) and sharing features.

3.2 AI-Powered Personalization

  • Food images are sent to our AI service provider (OpenRouter API) for automated food recognition and calorie estimation. Images are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored by the AI provider.
  • The "Trainer Sisir" AI coaching feature uses your profile data (age, weight, height, goals, dietary preferences, allergies), daily calorie/macro intake, exercise data, and health metrics to provide personalized nutrition coaching.
  • AI chat conversations are stored in your Firebase account to maintain context and conversation history.
  • AI models used: OpenAI GPT-4o-mini (primary food recognition), Google Gemini Flash 1.5, Anthropic Claude 3 Haiku (fallback vision models), and OpenAI GPT-3.5-turbo (chat coaching) — all accessed through the OpenRouter API gateway.

3.3 Communications

  • Send push notifications about meal reminders, water intake reminders, goal progress, and achievements (with your consent via device settings).
  • Respond to customer support inquiries.
  • Send important account and service-related notices.

3.4 Analytics & Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns via Firebase Analytics to improve App features and user experience.
  • Monitor and resolve crashes and technical issues via Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Generate anonymized, aggregated data for internal research and development.

5. Apple HealthKit Compliance

Apple HealthKit Specific Disclosures: In compliance with Apple’s HealthKit guidelines and App Store Review Guidelines, we confirm the following:

  • Data obtained through HealthKit APIs is not used for advertising, marketing, or data mining purposes.
  • HealthKit data is not sold to advertising platforms, data brokers, or information resellers.
  • HealthKit data is not shared with third parties without your explicit consent, except as required to provide core App functionality.
  • HealthKit data is not used to build user profiles for purposes unrelated to health and fitness.
  • HealthKit data is used exclusively to provide health and fitness features within the App, including displaying activity data, calculating calorie balance, and powering AI coaching insights.
  • The App does not write any data back to HealthKit — access is read-only.

6. Data Security and Protection

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses industry-standard TLS/SSL encryption.
  • Encryption at Rest: Personal information stored in Firebase is encrypted using AES-256 encryption on Google Cloud Platform.
  • Secure Authentication: Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. OAuth 2.0 is used for Google and Apple Sign-In.
  • API Security: AI service API keys are stored server-side in Firestore and are never hardcoded or exposed in the client application.
  • Access Controls: Role-based access control and principle of least privilege for all system access.
  • Infrastructure: Hosted on Google Cloud Platform (Firebase) with SOC 2, ISO 27001, and GDPR compliance certifications.
  • Local Security: Sensitive data cached on your device is stored in secure local storage (SharedPreferences, SQLite with system-level encryption).

While we implement robust security measures, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. You acknowledge and accept the inherent security risks of internet-based services.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

7.1 Retention Periods

  • Account Data: Retained as long as your account is active.
  • Health & Fitness Data: Retained to maintain your history unless you request deletion.
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated analytics retained for 26 months (Firebase default).
  • Crash Logs: Retained for 90 days for debugging.
  • AI-Processed Images: Not retained by the AI provider; processed in real-time only.

7.2 Your Right to Delete Data

  • You can delete your account at any time through the App (Settings → Account → Delete Account).
  • Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • Backup copies are deleted within 30 days following our backup retention schedule.
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance (e.g., fraud prevention) for up to 7 years as required by applicable law.
  • Anonymized, aggregated data that cannot identify you may be retained for analytics purposes.

7.3 How to Request Data Deletion

  • Delete your account through the App settings (recommended, immediate).
  • Email us at calorievita@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request."
  • We will confirm receipt within 72 hours and complete the deletion within 30 days.
